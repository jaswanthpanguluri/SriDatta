About Countryoven

For Over 30 years, CountryOven (A unit of Karthik Enterprises Private Limited) helping customers to celebrate their special moments by delivering yummy cakes and fresh flowers.

Our Oven bakes joy!

Running from one store to the other to find the perfect cake or box of chocolates or anything for that matter is troublesome. Not being able to find what you were exactly looking for can be frustrating. Country Oven has been solving such crises for plenty of years now, bringing smiles to doors as and when needed not just through cakes but also through flowers, sweets, and gifts.

Our online portal started with the comprehension that the items on the table play a major role in making special occasions truly special since friends and family bond while enjoying these items. We, thus, formed a team to deliver just that in a highly convenient and quick manner. Whether you want it delivered in the middle of the day or the middle of the night, we are here to cater to your needs.

On countryoven.com we offer a wide variety of items that are sure to bring joy – cakes to gifts, we have them all for you! We have taken the efforts to ensure that everything is in one place for you so that you don’t have to hunt around in different places. With the convenience of our defined time slots as well as express delivery (one-day delivery) options on most of our products, you can expect to not just find what you need but also have it delivered exactly when you need it.

We also understand that you may have doubts, queries, or concerns about our products and our services, which is why we have devised a top-notch customer service panel, trained specifically to serve you, that will be at your beck and call at all times. We offer you a number of ways to contact us including live chat because we know that some problems need to be solved immediately. We are just one phone call/email/ping away no matter when you need us.

Country Oven has become a very popular name today and we strive each day to keep that intact, considering your valuable feedback and reviews, and improvising at every turn.
