Careers at Countryoven

Discover the taste of a rewarding career with Sri Datta.

Sri Datta (A unit of Karthik Enterprises Private Limited) is a leading chain of bakeries and restaurants in the twin cities, helping customers celebrate their special moments by delivering a wide range of cakes, gifts, and fresh flowers.
We are currently seeking dynamic, and experienced professionals for the following positions:

Name of Position Job Description
Marketing Executive:

The ideal candidate should be a graduate with at least 3 years of experience in marketing food products to large corporate cafeterias, food courts, hospitals, and event organizers. Excellent communication skills and a proven ability to work with corporate facility management teams are preferred.

Customer Support Executive:

As an e-commerce pioneer, customer satisfaction is crucial to our success. The applicant must be polite and possess exceptional communication skills to interact with NRIs, ensuring them a pleasant online experience. The role requires shift work and the CS Executive must also be ready to work on festival days and special occasions.

Chef – Bakery Snacks:

Candidates should have experience in preparing popular bakery snacks, including Veg. and Non-Veg Puffs, Rolls, Pizzas, Burgers, Samosas, Croissants, Muffins, etc.
Chef – Indian Cuisine:

Chefs experienced in preparing multi-cuisine dishes, including Biryani, Punjabi, Tandoori, and Mughlai items, are invited to apply for this position at our Mint Leaf Restaurant.
Food Delivery Associates:

Candidates must have their own two-wheeler and a valid driving license. Preference will be given to those with knowledge of Hyderabad city and experience in food delivery.
